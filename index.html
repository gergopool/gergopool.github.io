About

In the past few years, as computer vision has grown and gained popularity, I realized there's a high demand for more specialists in this field to automate, optimize and validate workflow and AI products. Therefore, I offer a range of services to help companies and individuals to implement accurate, fast and robust AI solutions successfully.

Services

Consulting

If you want to kick off a project, but you are not exactly sure what steps you should take to achieve your goals, I am here to give you helpful advice and overcome your problems. I particularly recommend taking advice as early as possible, because many clients take wrong direction in data accumulation and labelling which is the earliest step, but at the same time the most important part of machine learning project.

Design

A mid-way between consulting and development. I offer my expertise in the design of a python code and advise on what details might be important in the implementation of a robust code base. At this step, it's very important to formalize the system requirements with a list of available hardware.

E.g. real-time tracking of pedestrians in a park with a Raspberry-Pi 3B+

Development

If you got stuck with the implementation or simply need some help, I am here, ready to get my hands dirty and dive into the code. I code in Python, both in TensorFlow and Pytorch.

Modules I feel comfortable using: Pytorch, TensorFlow, Keras, OpenCV, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn, Django, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Following Google Python Style (yapf)
